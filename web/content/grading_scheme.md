# Grading Scheme

- Assignments: 30%
- Quizzes: 20% (2 Quizzes worth 10% each)
- Mid-Semester Exam: 15%
- End-Semester Exam: 25%
- Labs: 10%
- Bonus*: 5%

{{<hint info>}}
Note: Total score for a student would be capped at 100% (if the student's score happens to exceed 100)
{{</hint>}}
